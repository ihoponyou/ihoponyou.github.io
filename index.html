about me

hey there! my name is noah, and this is my website. why do i type in all lowercase? i'm not sure, but i think it looks just fine. anyway, i'm a sophomore computer science major. i originally planned on studying mechanical engineering, but i found out during my senior year of high school that i wasn't really sure why. long story short: i found out that i like to code--and i'm pretty good at it too.

as far as career aspirations, i want to make games. coding, modeling, animating, all of it (except maybe music). if i can't make that happen, then i'll find something that isn't too soul-draining. in that case, i'll probably make games on the side anyway.
